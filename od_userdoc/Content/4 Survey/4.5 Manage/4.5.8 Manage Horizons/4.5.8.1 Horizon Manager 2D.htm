4.5.8.1 Horizon Manager 2D

To open the Manage 2D Horizons window, navigate through Survey--> Manage--> Horizons--> 2D... or use the icon from the Manage toolbar. In the left panel of the window, the available horizons are displayed. It the bottom panel, information on the selected horizon is displayed (eg. location on disk, date last modified). At the base of the window the available disk space is noted.

Horizons can be renamed , locked , removed , copied , set as default or viewed as a dataset group .
