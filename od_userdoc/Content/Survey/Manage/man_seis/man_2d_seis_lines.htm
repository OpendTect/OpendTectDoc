Manage 2D Seismic Lines

Accessed via the 'Manage 2D SeismicsManage 2D Seismics' window, either by double-clicking on a dataset name or via the icon.

Lines can be renamed () and deleted (). The following actions can also be made on the lines: Merge of several lines to a new line, Extraction (projection) from 3D volumes along the 2D lines, Export of the geometry to GoogleEarth.
